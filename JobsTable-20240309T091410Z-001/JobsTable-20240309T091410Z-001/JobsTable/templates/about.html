About EduMatch

EduMatch is a web application designed to help students and admins connect with teaching assistant and tutor positions at Durban University of Technology. Our platform provides an easy-to-use interface for both students and admins to manage their applications and positions.It aims to streamline the process of finding suitable positions for both students and academic departments.

Our mission is to streamline the process of finding and applying for teaching assistant and tutor positions, making it simpler and more efficient for students and admins alike.
