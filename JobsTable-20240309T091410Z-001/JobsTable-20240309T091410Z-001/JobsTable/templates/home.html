About EduMatch+

EduMatch+ is a revolutionary web application designed to transform the way students and professionals connect with teaching assistant and tutor positions at Durban University of Technology. By seamlessly integrating cutting-edge technology with educational opportunities, EduMatch+ creates a dynamic platform for academic and career advancement.

Our mission is to innovate the traditional job search process by providing a user-friendly interface that matches candidates with the perfect job opportunities. Whether you're a student looking to gain valuable teaching experience or an academic department seeking top-tier talent, EduMatch+ is your gateway to success.

Tutoring and Teacher Assistant Opportunities

Students:

  • Application Portal: Students can apply for tutoring and TA positions.
  • Opportunity Access: Gain access to available positions.
  • Application Management: Easily manage and track applications.
  • Direct Connection: Directly connect with departments offering positions.

Departments:

  • Position Posting: Departments can post available tutoring and TA positions.
  • Applicant Management: Manage applications and select candidates.
  • Efficient Connection: Facilitates connecting with potential tutors and TAs.
  • Streamlined Process: Simplifies the process of finding qualified candidates.
